Sketching Starter

Basics

Start with simple shapes like circles, lines, and cubes. Focus on light pencil strokes and observation.

Tools

  • Graphite pencils (HB, 2B, 4B)
  • Eraser and blending stumps
  • Sketchbook or drawing pad

Daily Challenge

Draw one object from your room every day for the next 7 days. Quick 10-minute sketches!
